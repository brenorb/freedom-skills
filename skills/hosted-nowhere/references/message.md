# Message

Route: `https://hostednowhere.com/create/message`

Minimum viable content:

- sender name
- body text or title

Success cues:

- the Verification step renders a phrase for the page
- the Share Link step reveals a URL under `https://hostednowhere.com/s#...`

Important caution:

- a blank body is allowed only if the builder has a title tag

Good demo payload:

- sender name: `Signal Hill Dispatch`
- body: `Tonight's meetup has moved to the back room. Bring cash for tea.`
