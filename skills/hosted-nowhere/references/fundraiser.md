# Fundraiser

Route: `https://hostednowhere.com/create/fundraiser`

Minimum viable content:

- fundraiser name

Success cues:

- the Verification step shows a phrase for the page
- the Share Link step reveals a URL under `https://hostednowhere.com/s#...`

Important caution:

- the first publishable draft can be minimal, but real fundraising usually needs payment details and a clear purpose before sharing widely

Good demo payload:

- fundraiser name: `Community Mesh Fund`
