# Drop

Route: `https://hostednowhere.com/create/drop`

Minimum viable content:

- body text

Success cues:

- the Verification step shows a phrase for the page
- the Share Link step reveals a URL under `https://hostednowhere.com/s#...`

Important caution:

- a title is optional, but a blank body is not publishable

Good demo payload:

- body: `Leave food donations in the yellow locker after dusk.`
